Founded by The Muyingo Family, Seeta High Schools were first established in 2000 and celebrating 25 years and have since then expanded include four campuses_ Main, Mukono, Green, A'Level. We offer education based on the UNEB Curriculum while integrating faith since we are a Christian institution. All our have state-of-the-art facilities and skilled faculty members to ensure consistency and excellence in our students.

Our mission is to nurture young minds, equipping them with the knowledge, skills nad values they need to succeed in today's fast-changing world.
